Hi, I'm Tara,

a full stack software developer based in Columbus, Ohio.

My earlier professional background is in libraries and bookselling, and I'm especially interested in tech in libraries, publishing, and literature. Some of my other interests include letterpress printing and printmaking, translation, my dog and houseplants, and PC gaming.

A single-page Javascript web application for creative writers.
